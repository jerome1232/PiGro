PiGro

An (mostly) automated Greenhouse

PiGro is an automated Greenhouse. It uses an Arduino Uno to
read data from soil moisture sensors, a DHT11 temperature and
humidity sensor, and a simple photocell to read light levels.
Using data from these sensors it heats the greenhouse, opens a lid
to vent the greenhouse, waters dry plant bays, and turns on the
lights when it grows dark outside. Currently the only things you
need to do is keep it's water bucket filled, and enjoy your plants.
